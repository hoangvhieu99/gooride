Cycling Modes

Cyclist can track and save your cycling journeys with GooRide for FREE.

GooRide will record journey information such as: Distance, Speed, Time.

Your cycling history will be saved and shared at: Explore > My Journey

Cyclists can earn $RIDE token in Solo mode and they need to own at least 1 Bike NFT to start.
GooRide will tracks your location by using GPS, so you'll need to get outside.

You can earn money when you use your bike every day, when you ride an exercise bike or cycle to experience travel.

The special mode allows professional Cyclists to earn royalties by sharing experience information, a detailed experience of a track that a Cyclist has completed in the form of NFT.

Users can buy the copyright of NFT Tours to see full information about the tour that interests you. It's practical, useful information to prepare you well for your upcoming journey.

Connect and "Ride and Earn" together. Leader will be increased by 5%, members will be increased by 2% earning index.

The minimum number of members required to form a group is 5. Cyclists who do not own an NFT are not permitted to join the group.
